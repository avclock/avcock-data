# AvClock — Support

AvClock shows local times, airport & heliport information, and aviation
weather (METAR/TAF) for airports worldwide. **Reference only — not for
navigation or flight planning.**

## Contact
Questions, bugs, or feedback: **avclock@protonmail.com**
(We usually reply within a few days.)

## FAQ

**Why doesn't an airport show weather?**
Weather (METAR/TAF) is only available for airports with a reporting
station — generally those with an ICAO code. Many small fields don't report.

**Is the data official?**
No. Airport data comes from OurAirports (public domain) and weather from
NOAA. It is for reference only — always confirm with official sources
(FAA, NOTAMs, official charts, and ATC) before any aviation decision.

**What does AvClock Premium include?**
A one-time purchase (no subscription) that unlocks live weather, Flight
mode, the weather widget, Live Activities, aviation themes, custom app
icons, and more.

**How do I restore my purchase?**
Settings → Restore Purchases, using the same Apple Account you bought with.

**Privacy**
AvClock collects no personal data. See the Privacy Policy in the app or the
repository.
